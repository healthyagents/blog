About this blog

I started this blog to spread the word about health-related agent based modeling.

Agent based modeling is now widely used in a wide range of disciplines. However, it has been slower to diffuse in health-related areas. In this blog I will post about published agent-based models that help us better understand population health. I will also be posting about other innovative computational social science applications to health.

About me

My name is Cristina Metgher and I am a PhD candidate in Computational Social Science at George Mason University. I work on social determinants of cancer using agent based modeling. More about me here.
